Decide for yourself!

If you're in Venice, you can head to the Campanile in Piazza San Marco and follow along a short walking tour to uncover the "dov'era" debate: should the tower have been rebuilt in the same spot, or would it look better somewhere else? Walk around the Campanile and the Piazza and take your own position.


The tour starts in front of the entrance to the Campanile. If you see a line-up to go up the tower, you're probably in the right place. See the Google Map below on this page to find the starting point. Once you're ready, click on the audio track to start the tour. See you there!
